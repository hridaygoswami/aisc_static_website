about

Explore ideas with learning.

At Adelaide Institute of Science (AISC), we are dedicated to fostering academic excellence and professional growth through our specialized programs in Human Services and Business Administration. Located in Adelaide, Australia, AISC is a dynamic and student-focused institution that prepares both domestic and international students for impactful careers. Our commitment to innovation, industry relevance, and student success makes AISC the ideal destination for higher education.

Elevating Education, Enriching Communities

Why Choose AISC?

Student-Centered Learning: Flexible study options, including face-to-face, blended, and online learning to suit diverse needs.
Industry-Relevant Curriculum: Developed in collaboration with industry experts to ensure graduates are job-ready with real-world skills.
Global Recognition: Qualifications accredited and recognized by Australian and international employers.
Support for International Students: Comprehensive support services, visa assistance, and career guidance for a smooth transition.
Career Placement & Internships: Strong industry partnerships provide internships, work placements, and networking opportunities.
State-of-the-Art Campus & Facilities: Cutting-edge learning environments equipped with modern technologies for an enhanced educational experience.

A Closer Look at AISC & 

Its Impact

Our goal is to deliver and end to end range of educational and learning services that opens jobs and opportunities for our students. AISC is a pathway for students to their choice of education, learning and careers. At AISC we provide quality education from a new perspective. Our qualified and experience lecturers are excited to work with each individual student to bring progress and productiveness in his or her learning experience. Once our students successfully attain their qualification degree, our services further extend to provide them with assistance in entering the industry. This will be in the form an industry placement in fast developing industry in the Australian market. We believe in todays students are tomorrows future, accordingly we aim to be able to firstly educate our students for the choice of career, then prepare them for the challenges and obstacles that are involved in todays workplace and culture and furthermore we create an entrance for the particular student in their field of study. We look to be known as the angels of the education and learning.
